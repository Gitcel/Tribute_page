Michael Joe Jackson

1960 - 2009

American singer, songwriter and dancer. He was the "King of Pop" and one of the most famous entertainers in the world, as well as one of the best-selling musicians of all time. Jackson's contributions, along with his publicized personal life made him a global figure in popular culture for more than four decades.

  • 2002 - American Music Awards "Artist of the Century"
  • 2001 - Michael Jackson 30th Anniversary Concerts
  • 1997 and 2001 - Rock & Roll Hall of Fame
  • 1993 - Grammy Living Legend Award
  • 1993 - Super Bowl 27 Performance
  • 1985 - We Are the World" released
  • 1984 - Victory Tour
  • 1984 - Record Eight Grammy Awards
  • 1983 - Motown 25 - Yesterday, Today, Forever
  • 1970 - Jackson Five debuts with four number one Hits.
